Brazil sets August 2026 target for online casino product regulation — what operators should prepare

The SPA has indicated that it intends to finalise the regulatory framework for online casino products by August 2026, extending the federal authorisation regime beyond sports betting into slots, table games and live dealer. Operators with existing federal authorisations should be preparing their product and compliance infrastructure now.
